Sidecar is a function that allows you to use an iPad as a second display for your Mac. 
Once set up, you can move your pointer to the iPad. 
You can also edit a PowerPoint or Keynote presentation on the Mac while viewing it on the iPad. 

Siri is a funtion that allows you to search by voice anything on your computer. 
You can also ask Siri to search the web, and call/text people in your contacts. 
Siri can also be used to search for documents on your computer. 

Keychain is basically a password manager for Mac OS. 
Catalina added the ability to also integrate passwords saved in Google Chrome to use on all devices. 
It is a cloud based function as well, so that you can take your passwords to new (Apple) devices, as well as use them across all current devices.
